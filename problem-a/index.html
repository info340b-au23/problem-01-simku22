Zeus - (Chief deity of the pantheon)

Zeus, a sky and weather god, was the centre of many ancient Greek and Cretan myths. Within Greek Mythology, he is a "Father" to all gods and humans alike. He is associated with stormy and cloudy weather, and often wields lightning and thunder as a symbol of protection and power.

Zeus's Many Talents:

  • The ability to quite literally wield lightning bolts
  • Performed some pretty epic justice upon Sisyphus, who will be rolling a boulder up a hill for eternity.
  • Successfully averted insurrection attempts by lesser gods multiple times
